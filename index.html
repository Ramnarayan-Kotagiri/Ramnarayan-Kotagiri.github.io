01 Engineering direction

From software products to intelligent field systems.

My foundation is in web, mobile, cloud, search, automation, and applied image-processing research. I am directing that experience toward digital tools that help people understand and manage living agricultural environments.

An agricultural digital twin is a continuously updated relationship between a physical plantation, observations, structured data, models, and human decisions. The work here separates what I have built from the system I am building toward.

09 About

Software engineering grounded in useful systems.

I am Ramnarayan Kotagiri, a JavaScript and full-stack engineer with experience spanning Angular applications, mobile products, backend services, cloud delivery, search, automation, and applied research.

My GitHub profile has long stated an interest in bringing digital capabilities to small-scale farmers. Oil palm cultivation software gave that direction a concrete domain. I am now developing the geospatial, sensing, imagery, and machine-learning perspective needed to think beyond static information tools and toward living digital systems.

I am interested in work where software has to respect field context, uncertain data, and the judgment of the people making decisions.
